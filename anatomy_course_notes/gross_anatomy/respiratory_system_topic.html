Gross Anatomy - Course Notes

Topic: Skeletal System

The skeletal system provides structure and support to the human body.

1. Bones

Bones are rigid organs that form part of the endoskeleton. They support the body and protect internal organs.

2. Joints

Joints connect bones and allow for movement and flexibility.

3. Ligaments

Ligaments are strong fibrous tissues that connect bones to other bones at joints.

Topic: Muscular System

Muscles work with the skeletal system to help the body move.

1. Types of Muscles

There are three types of muscles: skeletal, smooth, and cardiac muscles.
